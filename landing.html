Local-first workflow

From raw capture to focused cut.

01

Open any screen recording

Use `.mov` or `.mp4` from QuickTime, OBS, Loom, or another recorder. Preview and editing happen in your browser on this machine.

02

Place attention deliberately

Move the playhead, choose Zoom or Text, then click the exact location in the video. Drag text directly to refine its position.

03

Render a portable MP4

The local server uses ffmpeg for H.264 video and AAC audio, with fast-start enabled for reliable sharing and playback.
